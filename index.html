We are:

>> The Ethical Hacking Club at Plymouth State University is a community of passionate cybersecurity enthusiasts dedicated to promoting ethical hacking practices, cybersecurity awareness, and responsible information security on campus. Our mission is to foster an environment where students can learn, collaborate, and share knowledge related to cybersecurity and ethical hacking.
>> Whether you're an experienced cybersecurity enthusiast or a beginner interested in learning about ethical hacking, the Ethical Hacking Club welcomes all levels of expertise. Join us in our mission to explore the fascinating world of cybersecurity, learn valuable skills, and make a positive impact on our college campus and beyond.
>> For more information and updates feel free to contact us at Jacob True, or join our discord. We look forward to welcoming you and meeting you!
